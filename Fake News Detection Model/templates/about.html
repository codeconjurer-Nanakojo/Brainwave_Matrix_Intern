About the Fake News Detector App

Overview

Our Fake News Detector app leverages advanced machine learning algorithms to identify and flag false information. Designed to combat misinformation, it provides users with reliable insights to help them discern the authenticity of the news they encounter.

Core Features

  • Real-time Analysis: Instantly analyze news articles and identify potential false information.
  • Machine Learning Algorithms: Utilizes state-of-the-art ML models for high accuracy in detecting fake news.
  • User Friendly GUI: The app's interface is design to give a friendly and clear user experience
